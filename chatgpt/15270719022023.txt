Q. Tell me something about Arun Tiwari

Arun Tiwari is an Indian author, entrepreneur, and motivational speaker. He is best known for his book, You Can Win, which has sold over 3 million copies worldwide. He is also the founder of the Arun Tiwari Foundation, which works to empower and inspire people to reach their full potential. He has been featured in various publications such as The Times of India, The Hindu, and The Economic Times. He has also been a guest speaker at various events and conferences.

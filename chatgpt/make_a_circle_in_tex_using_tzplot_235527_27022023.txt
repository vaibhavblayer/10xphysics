Q. Make a circle in tex using tzplot

\begin{tikzpicture}
\begin{axis}[
    axis lines=middle,
    xmin=-2,xmax=2,
    ymin=-2,ymax=2,
    xlabel=$x$,
    ylabel=$y$,
    xtick={-2,-1,0,1,2},
    ytick={-2,-1,0,1,2},
    xlabel style={below right},
    ylabel style={above left},
    axis line style={->},
    samples=100,
    domain=-2:2
]
\addplot[
    thick,
    color=blue,
    domain=-2:2,
    samples=100,
]
{sqrt(4-x^2)};
\addplot[
    thick,
    color=blue,
    domain=-2:2,
    samples=100,
]
{-sqrt(4-x^2)};
\end{axis}
\end{tikzpicture}

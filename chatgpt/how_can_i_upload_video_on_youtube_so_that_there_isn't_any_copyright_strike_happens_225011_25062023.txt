Q. How can i upload video on youtube so that there isn't any copyright strike happens

1. Make sure you own the copyright to the video or have permission from the copyright holder to upload it.

2. Use YouTube's copyright-free music library or create your own music to use in the video.

3. Make sure you have permission to use any images, logos, or other copyrighted material in the video.

4. Use YouTube's Content ID system to protect your video from copyright claims.

5. If you receive a copyright claim, respond to it quickly and follow YouTube's instructions.

6. If you receive a copyright strike, appeal it and provide evidence that you own the copyright or have permission from the copyright holder.

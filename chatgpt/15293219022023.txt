Q. Tell me something about Vaibhav Blayer

Vaibhav Blayer is an Indian entrepreneur, investor, and philanthropist. He is the founder and CEO of the venture capital firm Blayer Ventures, which invests in early-stage startups. He is also the founder of the Blayer Foundation, a non-profit organization that works to promote education and entrepreneurship in India. He is a member of the Young Presidents' Organization (YPO) and a mentor at the Indian School of Business (ISB).

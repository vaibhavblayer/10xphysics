Q. Tell me something about Raja Ram Vishnoi

Raja Ram Vishnoi is a prominent Indian politician and social worker from Rajasthan. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and is currently serving as the Member of Parliament from the Nagaur constituency in Rajasthan. He is a strong advocate of social justice and has been actively involved in various social causes in his constituency. He has also been instrumental in the development of his constituency and has been credited with bringing about various development projects in the area.
